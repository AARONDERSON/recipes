Buffalo Chicken Wing Dip

Description

Invented in 1964 after Dominic Bellisimo arrived home unannounced with some of his friends from school.
Instantly becoming a East Coast staple. His mother, Teressa Bellisimo, and father Frank Bellisimo owned
a bar, the Anchor Bar in Buffalo, New York.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Cream Cheese
  • Ranch Dressing
  • Hotsauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat chicken and hot pepper sauce in a skillet over medium heat until heated
    through, 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in cream cheese and ranch dressing. Cook and stir
    until well blended and warm, 3 to 5 minutes.


  2. Mix in 1/2 of the Cheddar cheese, and transfer the mixture to a slow cooker.
    Sprinkle the remaining cheese over top.


  3. Cover and cook on low until dip is hot and bubbly, about 35 minutes.


  4. Serve with crackers and or chips.
