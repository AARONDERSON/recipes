Loaded Chili Potato Skins

Description

An exciting upgrade to the classic potato skin with simple and easy to read instructions.
Perfect for game day, family get togethers, or watching 8 seasons of game of thrones.

Ingredients

  • 8 small (3 to 4 inches) russet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Coarse salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 1 (15 ounce) can HORMEL® Chili With Beans
  • 2 cups of sharp Cheddar Cheese
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • ½ cup sliced green onions
